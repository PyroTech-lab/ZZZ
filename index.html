Part 1: Presentation


#1 Contract and payment

We have sent a contract for you to sign as well as a payment link in the onboarding email.
It is the first step to start working with us and we will be able to start creating your advertising campaign only after this step is completed.

#2 Our services

As agreed in the contract, our services consist of setting up your campaign, and then managing it.

The setup services consist of:

-Setting up your Facebook Business Manager
-Creating a Facebook Advertising campaign
-Creating a Landing Page to capture leads into a sales funnel
-Setting up an automated marketing system (consisting of email & SMS) to retain and create leads

The management services consist of:

-Managing and optimizing your Facebook campaign daily.
-Managing and optimizing your retargeting campaigns daily.
-Optimizing and refining the sales funnel using the collected data.
-Forwarding leads (calls & opt-ins) to your company.

#3 Timeline

Once you have completed the setup process, it will take 3-4 business days to finish setting up your campaign.

After your campaign is set up, we will start testing different ad sets with low budgets. The testing phase lasts generally 2 days.
Once the testing phase is completed, we will gradually scale up the advertising budget to generate the maximum amount of leads.

Even after the campaign is scaled up, we will continue optimising and testing different ad sets to minimise advertising cost and maximise reach.

Part 2: Setting up your profile


#1 Creating a Facebook Business Manager account

If you already have a Facebook Business Manager account, you can skip this step.

In order for us to run an advertising campaign for you, we need to connect to your Facebook Account.
Luckily, Facebook has developed a way for marketing agencies to manage ads for their clients without directly accessing their accounts.

To use this feature, the first step is to create a Facebook Business Manager account.
1) Go to www.business.facebook.com
2) Click on Create an account

3) Fill out your information

#2 Creating an ad account

If you already have a Facebook Business Manager account with its own ad account, you can skip this step.

You now have to create an ad account from were the adverts will be run.

1) Click on the Menu widget located on the top left corner of your screen

2) Click on Business Settings

3) Click on Ad accounts button located on the left of your screen

4) Click on Create a new ad account

5) Add your information. Give your business name to the ad account

6) Select This account will be used for another business or client and enter the Star Digital's business ID: 200679612497112

7) Select Luca van de Bilt or Star Digital Agency on the top left of the box and give full control of the ad account.

#3 Adding a payment method

Once you have created an ad account, the next step is to add a payment method to be able to purchase the advertisements.

1) Click on the Menu widget located on the top left corner of your screen

2) Click on Business Settings

3) Click on Payment Methods button located on the bottom left of your screen

4) Add a payment method to your Facebook Business Manager Account

#4 Adding a page

The penultimate step of your setup is to add a page to your Business Manager account.

1) Click on the Menu widget located on the top left corner of your screen

2) Click on Business Settings

3) Click on the Pages button located on the left side of your screen

4) Click on Add a Page

5) Find your page and click on Add Page

#5 Adding Star Digital as a Partner

The last step of the setup on Facebook is to add Star Digital as a partner.

1) Click on the Menu widget located on the top left corner of your screen

2) Click on Business Settings

3) Click on Partners button located on the left side of your screen

4) Click on Partner to share assets with

5) Enter Star Digital's business ID: 200679612497112

6) Select your page and give full control of the page

7) Select your ad account and give full control of the ad account
